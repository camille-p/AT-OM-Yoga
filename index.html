About Us

In our studio, classes take place in different languages. Newcomers to Ireland who don’t feel comfortable with English yet, or homesick expatriates missing their native language, you are all welcome to our practice. The languages we offer at the moment are French, Brazilian Portuguese, German, Spanish, and Irish and English as we are in Ireland after all! We also know what it feels like being a student and trying to decide between yoga classes or groceries, which is why we offer you guys discounts!
